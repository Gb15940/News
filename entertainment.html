    Youtubers

    Youtubers have always been a topic that's been passed over, mostly because they're just sitting on this one platform and not expanding from gaming or reaction videos. However, that has changed drastically.

    Jacksepticeye

    Irish youtuber Jacksepticeye, or Sean Mcloughlin, has been a youtube and teen icon for years.
    He has been a youtuber for years, but has expanded into his own business, Top Of The Mornin Coffee, he has now made his own work in progress game, The Overnight Watch, and for years he has held a charity event called Thankmas.
    He has raised countless donations for the charity, constantly lifts spirits up, and all in all is an amazing person.

    Markiplier

    Markiplier, Mark Fischbach, is another youtuber of around the same subscriber count, and possibly even the same affect on people like Sean.
    He has also expanded his career far beyond just being a youtuber, while also having made a few other channels during his time.
    Mark has made podcasts, like Distractible, and The Edge of Sleep that later turned into a tv series on amazon. He has now also made his own movie Iron Lung, which was based on an indie horror game he had played about a year earlier, and owns his own joint clothing company with Jacksepticeye, called CLOAK.
